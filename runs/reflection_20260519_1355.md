Why no improvement after many iterations:
1. Official best is a cache/composition of already-discovered best details for 8/10 cases plus hard seed401/low outputs. Many local changes do not alter hidden official output.
2. Low501 official history is exhausted: all saved submissions show the same 30 K=2 pairs and same costs. Picker/timing changes are no-op unless they generate a genuinely new low structure.
3. Seed401 official history/repacking is exhausted or dangerous: same-coverage and T0033 families repeatedly hit missing `T0028,T0033` and regress to `1589.3393`.
4. Synthetic proxies are useful for detecting instability, but their low cost scale does not match official low; single proxy improvements are not reliable.
5. Recombination/HGS-style mechanisms need diverse better columns. Existing internal solution pool lacks those columns, so recombination is no-op or worse.
6. KDD/RL value-function ideas map to opportunity costs, but a direct static courier shadow price worsened low proxy.

Implication:
- A real breakthrough likely requires a new generator for hidden low/scarce columns, not another picker/guard/cache.
- Any deeper refactor should preserve current caches and build an isolated alternative solver path that only activates when it beats current output under strict multi-model gates.

Next research targets:
- Exact/near-exact min-cost set packing for low using generated task-pair bundles with K=3/K=4 couriers under 60-courier budget.
- Stronger official-like low simulator from row-distribution fingerprints, not simple willingness scaling.
- Learning-inspired operator selection only after collecting local signatures; avoid direct stochastic final decisions.
